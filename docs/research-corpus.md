# Agent-Research: the platform's research corpus

The curated body of work the brain reasons from. Each entry notes how it grounds
the platform. Research lives in the innovation lab; only validated findings are
promoted (see `metamodels/research.agent`, `metamodels/theory.agent`, and the
constitution rules `research`, `evaluateAgainstGraph`, `researchWorkingGroup`).

## Agents — evolution, value, and small models

- **Self-evolving agents** — arXiv:2507.21046, arXiv:2508.07407. Agents that
  improve under governance → the infinite improvement loop, plateau-stop, and the
  sandboxed innovation lab.
- **Value learning** — arXiv:1308.0702. Learning objectives/values → alignment and
  the trust/decision layers.
- **QAIXI** — arXiv:2505.21170. Universal/quantum-aware agent intelligence →
  quantum-ready posture.
- **TinyLLM** — arXiv:2412.15304. Small, local models → the zero-cost floor,
  edge/device inference, the cost-aware model router.

## Graph — substrate, blockchain, federation

- **Blockchain: A Graph Primer** — Akcora, Gel & Kantarcioglu, arXiv:1708.08749.
  Blockchain *is* a graph; append-only/immutable history; smart-contract as a
  template for future edges; DAO as autonomous agent. → `theory.agent` ledger
  vocabulary; permissioned, not proof-of-work.
- **On Kubernetes-aided Federated Database Systems** — Li, Saldías-Vallejos,
  Rodríguez & Rainer, arXiv:2211.00373. The *fractal object* of data management;
  bottom-up federation; read-only/immutable container-native DBs. → recursive
  metamodel object graph, fractal, federated-over-centralized.
- **Learning Feynman Diagrams using Graph Neural Networks** — Mitchell, Norcliffe
  & Liò, arXiv:2211.15348. GNNs over diagram graphs → "the brain is the graph";
  link prediction for unknown-edge discovery.

## Decision intelligence and human–AI collaboration

- **Enhancing AI-Human Collaborative Decision-Making in Industry 4.0** — Alam &
  Khan, IEEE Access 2024, doi:10.1109/ACCESS.2024.3449415. Trust-calibrated,
  feedback-tuned weighted blend of human and AI utility. → `decision.agent` hybrid
  vocabulary; the decision-intelligence north star; community/SME human-in-the-loop.

## Economics

- **Principles of Economics** — Mankiw. Tradeoffs, opportunity cost, marginal
  thinking, incentives → `adapt`/`optimize`/`reduce`.
- **The Wealth of Nations** — Smith (1776). Division of labor, markets, the
  invisible hand.
- **The Use of Knowledge in Society** — Hayek (1945). Price signals coordinate
  dispersed knowledge with no central order → cost-signal model selection.
- **Exchange and Production (University Economics)** — Alchian & Allen. The
  Alchian–Allen effect: fixed per-call overhead biases selection toward quality.
- **Macroeconomics and Reality** — Sims (1980). Classical structural assumptions
  rarely hold → assume non-stationarity; selection is a bandit.
- **A Modern Approach to Probability Theory** — Fristedt & Gray,
  doi:10.1007/978-1-4899-2837-5. Laws of large numbers, convergence, stationarity →
  conformance stated with confidence, not asserted as "optimal."
- **Information / Financial / Planned economics** (surveys). Asymmetry, signaling,
  screening, adverse selection, moral hazard → trust, eval, governance, provenance;
  the plan-vs-price tension resolved by the governed brain + market-driven cortex.
