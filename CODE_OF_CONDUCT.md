# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/),
version 2.1.

## Our pledge
We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation a
harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or
invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and
expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality,
personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards
Examples of positive behavior: empathy and kindness, respect for differing
opinions, graceful acceptance of constructive feedback, focusing on what is best
for the community.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, trolling, insulting comments,
publishing others' private information, and other conduct reasonably considered
inappropriate.

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive or unacceptable behavior may be reported to the maintainers
at **conduct@unboxd.cloud**. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated
promptly and fairly. Maintainers are obligated to respect the privacy and
security of the reporter.

The full text and enforcement guidelines are available at
https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/.
